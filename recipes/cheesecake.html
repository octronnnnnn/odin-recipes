Baked Vanilla Cheesecake

How to make a classic baked Vanilla Cheesecake

The smooth and creamy Cheesecake, with a hint of vanilla, is a classic that's hard to beat. Making this Cheesecake requires only a few ingredients and is easier to make than you think. You'll find a full list of ingredients and a step by step guide below.

Ingredients

  • 250g biscuits
  • 140g butter, melted
  • 500g cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 300g Light Sour Cream
  • 155g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tsp vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Line the base of a springform pan with baking paper, allowing the edges to overhang. Secure the base into the pan.
  2. rush the biscuits in a food processor until finely crushed. Add the butter and process until well combined and place it on the lined pan. Even it out with the back of a spoon
  3. Meanwhile preheat the oven to 160Â°C. Place the cream cheese, sour cream and sugar in a clean bowl of a food processor and process until smooth. Add the eggs and vanilla, and process until well combined.
  4. our the cream cheese mixture into the prepared pan. Bake in the oven for 1 hour or until just set in the centre. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake in the oven, with the door slightly ajar, until cooled completely. Place in the fridge for 4 hours to chill.
